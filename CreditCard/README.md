 Credit Card Fraud Detection – Transaction-Level Analysis

Analyzes 20,000 transactions from fraudTrain.csv

Extracts fraud patterns by hour, merchant, category, and state

Visualizes fraud spikes and calculates fraud rates

Supports merchant-level fraud mitigation

Credit Card Fraud Risk Scoring – Individual Card Profiling

Uses CreditCardFraud.csv to assess fraud risk per card

Flags high-risk cards with scores > 0.5

Profiles cards with elevated or perfect fraud scores

Enables prioritization for fraud investigation teams

Techniques Used

Quantile-based risk flagging

KMeans clustering with warning-free setup

Group-wise fraud rate analysis

Time-based fraud pattern extraction

Score distribution profiling

Business Impact

Helps lenders and merchants identify high-risk customers and cards

Supports fraud prevention teams with targeted insights

Enables segmentation for personalized financial strategies

Tech Stack

Python, Pandas, NumPy

Matplotlib, Seaborn

scikit-learn (KMeans)

Jupyter Notebook
